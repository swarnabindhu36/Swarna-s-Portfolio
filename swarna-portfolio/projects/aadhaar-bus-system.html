Project · Smart India Hackathon 2024

Aadhaar-Enabled Automated Bus Transportation System

Hackathon Smart Systems

Overview

A smart transportation solution built for Smart India Hackathon 2024, integrating Aadhaar-based passenger authentication with digital ticketing and automated travel management for public bus systems.

Problem Statement

Conventional bus ticketing systems rely on manual verification and paper or basic digital tickets, which can be slow, error-prone, and difficult to audit. A more secure, automated approach to passenger verification and ticketing was needed.

Objectives

  • Use Aadhaar-based authentication to securely verify passenger identity.
  • Replace manual ticketing with a digital, automated process.
  • Enable automated tracking of passenger travel for better transport management.

Features

  • Aadhaar-based passenger authentication.
  • Digital ticketing that replaces manual, paper-based processes.
  • Passenger verification at the point of boarding.
  • Automated tracking of travel for operational visibility.

Technologies Used

Aadhaar AuthenticationDigital TicketingSmart Transportation Systems

Architecture / Workflow

  • Passenger identity is verified through Aadhaar-based authentication.
  • A digital ticket is issued automatically upon successful verification.
  • The system records and tracks the passenger's travel automatically.

My Contribution

I worked as part of the team that designed and built this solution for the hackathon, contributing to the system's design and the implementation of the authentication and ticketing workflow.

Challenges Faced

  • Designing a secure authentication flow within a tight hackathon timeline.
  • Coordinating a multi-part system (authentication, ticketing, tracking) as a team.

Learnings

  • Experience designing for real-world public infrastructure problems.
  • Strengthened teamwork, rapid prototyping, and technical problem-solving under time pressure.

Outcomes

A working prototype demonstrating Aadhaar-based authentication and automated digital ticketing, presented as part of Smart India Hackathon 2024.
