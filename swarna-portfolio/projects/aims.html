Audit Information Management System (AIMS)

Deployed Enterprise Software

Overview

AIMS is a web-based audit management application developed during my summer internship at Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (RINL). It streamlines audit-related workflows, document management, report handling, and information accessibility for internal audit teams.

Problem Statement

Manual audit processes involving scattered documents, reports, and records make it harder to track audit status and retrieve information efficiently. RINL needed a centralized system to manage audit information more reliably.

Objectives

  • Centralize audit-related documents, reports, and records in one application.
  • Streamline audit workflows from initiation to reporting.
  • Improve accessibility and retrieval of audit information for internal teams.

Features

  • Structured management of audit records and supporting documents.
  • Report handling and organized workflow tracking.
  • Designed for clarity and ease of access across the audit lifecycle.

Technologies Used

Web DevelopmentSystem DesignEnterprise Application Concepts

Architecture / Workflow

  • Audit records and documents are entered and stored within the system.
  • Workflows track each audit item through its reporting stages.
  • Authorized users retrieve and review audit information through the web interface.

My Contribution

I contributed to building the web application as part of the internship team — working on system design and development tasks that supported the audit workflow and document-handling features.

Challenges Faced

  • Understanding real enterprise audit workflows before translating them into software.
  • Coordinating development within a team in a live industry environment.

Learnings

  • Hands-on experience with real-world software development and system design.
  • Exposure to enterprise application concepts and how internal tools are actually used.
  • Improved team collaboration and problem-solving in a professional setting.

Outcomes

A functioning audit management system that improved how audit information was organized and accessed — and direct, real-world industry experience building enterprise software.
